About SkyClear.

SkyClear is a small analytics shop building the kind of tools we wish existed when we needed them.

Why we exist

Most of the big decisions in life — whether to buy a house, when to retire, how to price a business — get made with spreadsheets that one person built years ago and nobody else fully trusts, or with rules of thumb that may or may not apply to your situation. We think those decisions deserve better.

SkyClear tools are designed to be transparent about their assumptions, honest about their limits, and useful in plain English. No data-speak. No "trust me, I'm a quant." Just clearly-modeled answers to the questions you're actually asking.

How we work

We build two kinds of things:

  • Public tools like the Rent vs. Buy Calculator — free to use, designed for one specific decision.
  • Custom dashboards and models for clients who need analytics built around their specific data, business, or domain.

Every tool we publish goes through the same test: would a smart non-expert understand what's happening, trust the answer, and feel empowered to act on it? If not, we're not done.
